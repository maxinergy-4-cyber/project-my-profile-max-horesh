My Story.


I have been passionate about computers since I was a child my first computer was a Pentium 4, and that’s where it all started. Most of my time growing up, I immersed myself in gaming, which remains a big part of my life to this day. I enjoy streaming, creating my own content, setting up OBS, and developing projects around my games.

For most of my life, I wasn’t sure what I wanted to do professionally. I worked as a driver for several years, but over time I realized it didn’t fully use my abilities or passions. I’ve always been the person who jumps in to help others solve software problems, troubleshoot issues, and fix errors including problems I encountered myself and I don’t give up until the issue is resolved.

Recently, I discovered software testing (QA), and it immediately captured my interest. I started researching, asking questions, and exploring the field, and quickly realized it perfectly matches my skills and mindset. I am analytical, critical, and able to see things from multiple perspectives qualities I consider strong assets as a software tester.
